Executive Summary

Situation: Northwind Traders relied on a legacy on-prem MySQL database for both transactions and reporting, causing performance bottlenecks, slow reports, and limited scalability.

Task: Modernize the data stack on Google Cloud and deliver an end-to-end dimensional warehouse that reduces OLTP load, accelerates reporting, and enables self-service analytics.

Action:

  • Requirements: Identified 4 core processes (Sales Overview, Sales Agent Tracking, Product Inventory, Customer Reporting); profiled data for quality gaps.
  • Dimensional modelling: Kimball bus matrix with clearly declared grains; 4 conformed dimensions (customer, employee, product, date) and 3 fact tables (sales, inventory, purchase_order).
  • Architecture: Data Lake → Staging → Dimensional DWH → OBT in BigQuery; transformations orchestrated with dbt.
  • Data quality: SCD Type 2 where needed, null/duplicate handling in staging, generic tests + source freshness.
  • Performance: Partitioned facts by date; selective clustering/materializations; incremental models.
  • Visualization: Designed and built 3 interactive Tableau dashboards (self-initiated, not part of the bootcamp curriculum).

Result: A production-ready warehouse with clean dimensional models, automated dbt transformations, and executive-friendly dashboards—empowering stakeholders to analyze sales performance, monitor inventory health, and understand customer behaviour while removing strain from transactional systems.

How it’s Built

Warehouse (BigQuery)

  • Kimball star schemas with conformed dims & surrogate keys; clear naming.
  • 4-layer flow: Data Lake → Staging → DWH → OBT with separation of concerns.
  • Performance/cost: partitioning & clustering; selective materializations.

Transformations (dbt)

  • Reusable macros, modular CTE patterns; incremental where appropriate.
  • Tests: not_null, unique, relationships; source freshness; contracts & docs.
  • Consolidated Sales Overview & Agent into a single OBT to reduce redundancy.

Visualization (Tableau)

  • Clean KPI strip with accessible typography and strong interaction patterns.
  • Executive-ready views for sales, inventory, and customers.
  • Exposures link dashboards back to dbt lineage.

Dashboards

Sales Overview — Executive Pulse

KPI strip, interactive sales trend with date picker, agent performance heatmap, and Top-10 products to spotlight growth levers. Key metrics include total sales ($68K), AOV $1,703, order volume, and agent breakdowns.

Product Inventory — Stock Health

KPI strip, inventory health & coverage, sales by category, and a reorder queue to prioritize replenishment. Tracks on-hand units (1,641), SKU count (23), low-stock items (19), and health distribution.

Customer Overview — Growth & Retention

KPI strip, sales by employee, order frequency, and a customer map to target outreach and expansion. Includes order completion rate (93%), top customers by volume, geo spread, and employee breakdowns.
