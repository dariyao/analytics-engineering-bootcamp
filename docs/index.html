Featured Project — Northwind Analytics Warehouse

I rebuilt the classic Northwind OLTP dataset into an analytics-ready warehouse and interactive dashboards.

  • Dimensional model (facts & dimensions) in BigQuery.
  • Transformations orchestrated with dbt (staging → marts/OBT), plus tests & docs.
  • Three Tableau views: Sales Overview, Product Inventory, Customer Overview.

How it’s built

Warehouse

  • BigQuery star schema tuned for analysis.
  • Clear separation: raw → staging → marts/OBT.
  • Documented models for quick onboarding.

Transformations

  • dbt models with reusable macros.
  • Tests for not null, uniqueness, and relationships.
  • Version-controlled SQL; auto docs.

Visualization

  • Tableau dashboards with clear tooltips & filters.
  • Sales, inventory, and customer behavior views.
  • Consistent, accessible design system.

Dashboards

Product Inventory
